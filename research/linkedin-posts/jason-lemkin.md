## Key Learnings

- **Build a media engine around one market.** SaaStr stays focused on SaaS, AI, GTM, and founder problems.
- **Use repeated formats.** Q&A, lists, interviews, event recaps, and benchmark posts create a repeatable publishing rhythm.
- **Be direct and opinionated.** Jason's style shows that strong points of view can make B2B content more memorable.
- **Tie content to community.** SaaStr uses content to feed events, workshops, podcasts, and community participation.
- **Make stage-specific advice.** Founder content is stronger when it names the ARR stage, team size, or growth context.
