# Research Sources

## Topic
LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Objective

The objective of this project is to study how leading B2B SaaS marketers use LinkedIn to build their personal brand, educate buyers, generate demand, and create consistent organic growth. The research will be used to build a practical playbook for B2B SaaS marketing.
